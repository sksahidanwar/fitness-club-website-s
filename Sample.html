About Us


ASK Fitness Club was conceptualized keeping in mind the rationale behind multi specialty centre concept. At ASK club we integrate all the possible relaxation, exercises and group exercise techniques, i,e.,“gym, spa, yoga, aerobics” centers.are all under a single roof.

ASK Fitness Clubs are the luxury health clubs and salons launched to satisfy the niche segment by revitalizing their senses and soothe the jitteriness of city life.

ASK club is the first chain of health clubs to be awarded the ISO-9001-2000 and ISO-14001 certificates.
